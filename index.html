Green Campus Challenge

A simple project to help students build eco-friendly habits on campus.

Why it matters

Small actions like recycling, saving water, and reducing waste can make a big difference.

Weekly Challenges

Pick a few challenges each week and try to complete them.

  • Bring a reusable water bottle
  • Recycle paper and plastic
  • Turn off lights when leaving
  • Use stairs instead of elevator once
  • Use a reusable bag
